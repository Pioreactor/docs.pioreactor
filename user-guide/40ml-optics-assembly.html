Connect the optics system

Step 1: Necessary parts #

  1. You will need:
  • PD cables (2x)
  • IR cable (1x)
  • LED caps
tip

Inspect each IR and PD to confirm that their leads are not touching each other.

Step 2: Connect the IR cable (IR â†’ Channel A) #

  1. Secure LED caps over the X1 and X2 pockets on the vial holder.
  2. With the tab of the black connector pointed left, insert the bulb of the IR cable into the pocket labeled IR. See image.
  3. Slowly bend the bulb's leads 90 degrees towards the left. The black connector tab should be facing away.
caution

Avoid over-stressing the leads, or applying too much force too quickly. After bending once, it's ill-advised to bend them again.

  1. Secure the bulb with an LED cap.
  2. Insert the connector of the IR cable into channel A (labelled on the faceplate).

Step 3: Connect PD cable (REF â†’ Channel 1) #

  1. With the tab of the black connector pointed left, insert the bulb of one PD cable into the pocket labeled REF. See image.
  2. Slowly bend the bulb's leads 90 degrees towards the left. The black connector tab should be facing away.
  3. Secure the bulb with an LED cap.
  4. Insert the connector of the IR cable into PD channel 1 (labelled on the faceplate).
