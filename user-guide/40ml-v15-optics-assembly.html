Connect the optics system

Step 1: Necessary parts #

  1. You will need:
  • Eye-spy covers (3x)
  • 8mm screws (12x)
  • Eye-spies (2x)
tip

Each eye-spy is unique. The one used for REF has a white mark in the upper-left corner.

  • 100mm STEMMA-QT wire
  • 50mm STEMMA-QT wire
  • LED cable
  • LED caps (4x)

Step 2: Install the eye-spys #

  1. Place an eye-spy cover over the REF eye-spy.
tip

Each eye-spy is unique. The one used for REF has a white mark in the upper-left corner.

  1. With the tan connectors towards the top, place the eye-spy into the REF socket on the vial holder. Secure it with 4x 8mm screws.
  2. Place an eye-spy cover over the other eye-spy.
note

The measuring eye-spy can be placed in either the 90 or 135 degree pocket. For most applications, 90 degrees is the standard.

  1. Place the eye-spy into the desired pocket (90 or 135). Secure with 4x 8mm screws.
  2. Cover the remaining pocket with the last eye-spy cover. Secure with the remaining 8mm screws.

Step 3: Connect eye-spys to HAT #

  1. Locate the STEMMA-QT connector on the HAT and connect the 100mm STEMMA-QT wire, with the yellow wire furthest from the heater flat flex cable.
  2. Connect the other end to the leftmost eye-spy. Use the colors of the wire to orient the connector correctly.
  3. Use the 50mm STEMMA-QT wire to connect each eye-spy.
tip

It does not matter which wires are connected to either eye-spy sockets. However, organize the wires neatly and avoid crossing over when possible.

Step 4: Connect LED cable (Channel A â†’ IR pocket) #

  1. Connect the LED cable to the Channel A connector.
  2. Secure LED caps (3x) over the unused pockets.
  3. Insert the LED bulb into the pocket labeled IR. Slowly bend the bulb's leads 90 degrees towards the left such that the bend is near the LED bulb.
  4. Secure the bulb with an LED cap.
